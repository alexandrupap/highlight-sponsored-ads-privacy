Privacy Policy

Last updated: 09 Nov 2024

Overview

This Chrome extension, referred to as "the Extension," is designed solely to highlight sponsored ads on Google search result pages. It does not collect, store, or process any personal data or information from users.

Data Collection and Use

The Extension does not collect any personal or usage data. It functions entirely on your device and does not send any information to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

The Extension requires minimal permissions only to interact with the content of Google search result pages to highlight sponsored ads. This is necessary to achieve its functionality and does not involve any data collection.

Third-Party Services

No third-party services are used within the Extension.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Users will be notified of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
